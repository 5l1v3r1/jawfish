What is Jawfish?

Jawfish is a tool designed to break into web applications. This is called penetration testing in cybersecurity.

Based on Soen Vanned's Forced Evolution, it is self-modifying - finding exploits and updating its internal database accordingly.

Using Jawfish is as simple as giving it a target and condition of success. Parts to the targeting form are better explained on the next page.
